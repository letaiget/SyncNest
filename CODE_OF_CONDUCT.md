# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We aim to keep SyncNest welcoming, respectful, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus on ideas, not personalities.
- Accept feedback and collaborate in good faith.
- Help keep discussions productive.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or hateful language.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, remove content, or restrict access for violations.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces and public interactions related to SyncNest.
